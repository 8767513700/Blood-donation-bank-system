Why Donate Blood?

By donating blood, you can make a significant difference in the lives of patients in need. Your donation can help save lives, support surgeries, treat illnesses, and much more.

The Donation Process

  • Registration and eligibility check.
  • Health assessment and brief medical history review.
  • Blood donation, which usually takes about 10-15 minutes.
  • Rest and refreshments after donation.

Eligibility Criteria

Before donating blood, please ensure that you meet the eligibility criteria, including age, weight, and general health requirements.
