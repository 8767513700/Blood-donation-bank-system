Eligibility Criteria

Before you donate blood, it's essential to meet certain eligibility criteria. Please review the following to determine your eligibility:

  • You must be at least 17 years old.
  • You should weigh at least 110 pounds.
  • You should be in good general health.
  • You must not have certain medical conditions or take specific medications.
  • You should have a valid ID with you.

Temporary Deferrals

Sometimes, you may need to defer your donation temporarily. This can be due to various reasons, such as:

  • Recent travel to certain countries.
  • Recent medical procedures or vaccinations.
  • Low hemoglobin or iron levels.
